Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our bot and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Conversation Data: We collect messages and queries sent through Messenger to provide support, answer your questions, and improve the bot's responses.
  • Contact Details: We may store your contact information and conversation history to follow up on your inquiries.
  • Manual Payment Information: Because our payment process is manual (such as manual invoicing or bank transfers), we may collect billing details, contact info, or payment confirmations that you share with us to process your orders. We do not collect or store credit card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your queries and provide customer support.
  • To process and confirm your manual payments and coordinate your billing.
  • To maintain and improve our bot's performance.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • No Selling of Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.
  • Trusted Partners: We only share necessary details with trusted third parties as needed to process your manual payments (like banking institutions or invoicing tools) or to host our bot.

4. Data Retention & Your Rights

  • Retention: We only keep your chat history and payment records for as long as necessary to support you, complete transactions, or fulfill legal tax-reporting requirements.
  • Your Rights: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, or to ask that we delete your chat history.
